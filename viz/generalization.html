Transformer TPP with shared wallet embeddings across 25 markets
Explorer Single-Market Generalization
Result: A single Transformer TPP trained across 25 markets (9 cities, 483K events) achieves held-out LL of +9.17, dramatically beating per-market classical Hawkes (-5.29) on all 6 test markets. Shared wallet embeddings capture cross-market trading patterns — the same wallets exhibit consistent behavior across cities.
